Welcome to Ruse, Bulgaria

History of Ruse

Ancient Roots

Ruse has been a settlement since antiquity, known as Sexaginta Prista in Roman times.

Cultural Melting Pot

The city has been influenced by various cultures, including Roman, Ottoman, and Bulgarian.

Architectural Heritage

Ruse is famous for its beautiful architecture, featuring many buildings from the Renaissance and Baroque periods.

Modern Developments

Today, Ruse continues to grow and develop, maintaining its historical significance.
