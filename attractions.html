Liberty Monument

The Liberty Monument, located in the heart of Ruse, is a symbol of Bulgarian freedom and independence. It is known for its striking sculpture and prominent location in the city center.

Learn More

Ruse Museum of History

The Ruse Museum of History holds a fascinating collection of artifacts and exhibits that tell the story of the city and region. It is an essential stop for anyone interested in Bulgarian history.

Learn More

Rock-Hewn Churches of Ivanovo

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Rock-Hewn Churches of Ivanovo are a unique set of medieval churches and chapels carved into the cliffs near Ruse. These churches are famous for their well-preserved frescoes.

Learn More

Dohodno Zdanie (Revenue Building)

Dohodno Zdanie is an iconic theater and public building in Ruse known for its stunning Neo-Baroque architecture. It hosts a variety of cultural events and is a true architectural gem in the city.
